## 🎓 GOOD LUCK!

You have everything you need to excel on these exams. The key is:
1. **Extract first** (use the 15-minute protocol)
2. **Know your numbers** (specific HRs, CIs, p-values)
3. **Understand your methods** (why each statistical test)
4. **Link to the article** (say "Methods page X states...")

**The difference between average and excellent:**
- Average: "The study was a randomized trial"
- Excellent: "Stratified block randomization by ECOG and age, 1:1 allocation, central telephone randomization for concealment, achieving balance on all baseline characteristics (Table 1, all p > 0.05)"

Practice the extracted approach, and you'll naturally shift to excellent answers.
